Q: What is the minimum CGPA required for placements?
A: The minimum CGPA required is 6.5 for most companies. Some premium companies require 7.5 or above.

Q: Can students with backlogs sit for placements?
A: Students must have zero active backlogs at the time of company registration.

Q: Is attendance mandatory for placement training?
A: Yes, minimum 75% attendance in placement training sessions is compulsory.

Q: Can MBA students participate in tech placements?
A: No, MBA students are only eligible for management roles.
